WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

The timeframe of return can range from 14 days to 30 days or more from the date of purchase.The laptop should be in its original condition with all accessories, manuals, and packaging included. Customers are usually required to provide a reason for the return. Common reasons include receiving a defective product, not meeting expectations, or changing one's mind.

DO YOU PROVIDE DELIVERY SERVICES FOR ALL PRODUCTS?

We provide delivery services for all products in our website. The delivery fee ranges depending upon the distance of the product location from your location. Some products may also have free delivery charges. If any product is broken because of poor delivery, it is returnable by specifying the reason.

CAN I PRE-ORDER A SALE ITEM?

During major sale events like Black Friday, Cyber Monday, or holiday sales,Lappy.com offer pre-orders to allow customers to secure their desired laptops at the sale price before the event starts. This can be especially beneficial if the sale is expected to be in high demand and items might sell out quickly.
